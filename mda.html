Hospital Mobile Health Screening

overview

The client is one of the world’s most respected hospitals devoted exclusively to cancer patient care, research, education and prevention. The hospital conducts numerous research trials across multiple departments and divisions that need tracking.
The goal of these applications is to quickly assess a users health or eligibility based on a short form and quick validation. I chose to group these projects together because they are all for the same client, lightweight mobile apps to draw from each other.

role

UX / UI Designer
I was the sole designer on this project.
