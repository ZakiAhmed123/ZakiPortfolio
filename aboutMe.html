My journey and a little about me.

Hey there! I'm a UX designer living in Houston, Texas. I am an experienced UX leader with a 9-year track record in successful product delivery and team leadership. Skilled in user-centered design, UX research, product design, and design thinking workshops, I leverage my engineering background to bring a logical and analytical approach to decision making. As a former front-end developer, I effectively communicate with cross-functional teams, providing clear perspectives on timelines and feasibility. I foster teamwork and inclusivity to maximize output and encourage diverse creative ideas.
My story starts in December 2014 in Houston, Texas where I graduated with a bachelors degree in Petroleum Engineering. After having a hard time breaking into the oil and gas industry I tried a new approach and joined a coding bootcamp,
After learning the fundamentals of web development I started working in the startup space as a full-stack web developer. Shortly after moving a few jobs I found myself working as a consultant. My client needed some design work and I had some experience designing in the startup space so I tried my luck and fell in love with the process. Luckily I had an art background and have been painting and drawing my entire life so this felt like a happy marriage of my skillsets. After 9+ years of making the switch I am happy to say I am a design leader and have learned a lot through my years but have a lot more to learn.

One piece of advice

I have had multiple career changes in my life, many ups and many downs. One ideology I truly believe in is to be flexible, be malleable, adapt always, the tech space is constantly changing and as designers we have to as well.

Be flexible, adapt always.
