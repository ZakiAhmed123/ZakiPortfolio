Hospital Research Dashboard

overview

The client is one of the world’s most respected hospitals devoted exclusively to cancer patient care, research, education and prevention. The hospital conducts numerous research trials across multiple departments and divisions that need tracking.
The goal of this application was to create a multi-layered dashboard to allow department, division and hospital leadership to monitor and observe all research trials at a high level and be able to make decisions based on at a glance information.

UX / UI Designer
