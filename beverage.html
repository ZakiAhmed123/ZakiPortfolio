Beverage Company Helper App

overview

The client is one of the world’s largest carbonated drink manufacturers. The business has had a hard time communicating with its front line workers who stock shelves, talk directly with business that sell their product.
The goal of this application was to design a mobile and desktop application that allows the business unit of the beverage company to communicate documents, announcements and seasonal news to front line workers while also organizing their document library with versioning and robust search options.

role

UX Lead Designer
